Our Services

WHY  FIBERPRINT ?

FIBERPRINT offers advanced digital printing services which are at
high quality and affordable price for the T-shirt & garment products.

High Quality Digital Printing

High resolution printing and colour vibrancy.
Our printer capable of printing realistic photos,
water colour effect, 3D effect of full colour printing.

Industry Leader Equipments

We use the best technology from industry leader of
digial direct to garment printer to print your clothes.
Our printers, inks and blank t-shirts make your
impressions last and your products stands out.

No Minimum Order Required!

We accept order as low as 1 piece. Unlimited design
to print for per your order. We also accept large
quantity orders.

Multiple Garment Styles & Colours

We have variety of garments styles and colours for you
to choose from. Mens, ladies and youth/kids t-shirts.
We use only the best quality blank t-shirts such as
Gildan and Fruit of The Loom. We also accept your
own t-shirt and we will do only the printing.

Large Printing Size

With us you can print out your design from
A6, A5, A4, A3 and up to A2 size.
(594 x 420mm). That's BIG!

Delivery Worldwide

We can deliver your order either you in
Malaysia or overseas using POS Laju, Gdex,
Fedex, DHL and Sea Fright. You can
also self collect at our office
